Am I Eligible?

  • A student must be willing and able to compete.
  • A student must be enrolled in a degree program.
  • A student may compete for only one team during a contest.
  • A student who has won 5 or more contests is NOT eligible to compete..
  • A student who meets the requirments and was born in 1985 or later is eligible to compete.
Rules and Regulations
  • This is a team event. A team is composed of 2-3 programmers and a coach
  • Team composition is not tied to any institution. However, team composition cannot change when the contest kicks off.
  • There shall be no collaboration amongst other teams.
  • Any attempt to cheat will result in disqualification of the team
  • Participation is independent on programming plattform
